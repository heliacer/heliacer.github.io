History

It all started when I was a young pupil, attending a Scratch workshop at school. I was hooked, creating my account, fox_creations, and diving into the world of visual block programming. Instead of gaming, Scratch became my fixation, leading me to gain over 400 followers and create loads of games.

A year later, I got into Python, rebranded myself, and started dabbling in digital art for UI and logo designs. I also ventured into Discord, creating my own server and bots, mainly for organizing in-game competitions.

Then, I got curious about web development and had this wild idea for a Discord bot dashboard. I had no clue about hosting costs or GitHub, but I pushed through, learning frontend and backend development along the way.

Nowadays, I'm still on this journey, always looking to level up my skills and become a better developer than I was yesterday..
